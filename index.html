Location

Accomodation

We recommend one of two options.

1. Oakwood Cottages, Darver

There is limited accommodation available for our guests in Oakwood Cottages located just outside the main castle entrance approximately stumbling distance from Darver Castle.

These cottages contain 12 double en-suite rooms & there is a lobby area with tea and coffee facilities.

Breakfast is served the next morning in the Grain-store Restaurant at Darver Castle.

To enquire about the Guest Cottages, please contact Darver Castle directly;

  • Phone: +353 (0)42 9379466
  • Email: oakwoodcottages@darvercastle.ie

2. The Crown Plaza Hotel, Dundalk

Due to the limited accomodation around the Castle, we expect the majority of guests to stay in The Crown Plaza Hotel in Dundalk, only 4 miles away from Darver Castle.

The Crown Plaza provides a shuttle bus to & from Darver Castle on the day of the wedding so guests can leave their car at the hotel.

This will leave from outside the Crown Plaza Hotel on the afternoon of the wedding at approximately 2.30pm sharp to ensure guests arrive in time for the ceremony starting at 3pm.

The bus will then be on hand from around midnight to make trips back over to the hotel as required.

If you are planning on staying in The Crown Plaza - mention "McGee Wedding" when booking to avail of the special rate offered to our wedding guests.

  • Phone: +353 (0) 42 939 4900
  • Email: enquiries@crowneplazadundalk.ie
